How-to use

Just hit the space-bar or the unpause-button below to start. Every x millisecond (default: 1000ms) a new time appears. Solve the calculation as quickly as possible. Press enter to quickly submit your answer. Press space to toggle pause/unpause.

Input value is simplified: You don't have to separate minutes and seconds. 420 or 4.20 will be interpreted as 4:20 and 22 as 0:22.
